Hydration Nation

Hydration Nation is a fantastic program created and headed by Rob Machado, famous surfer and philanthropist. At Huntington Beach High School, we sell Hydration Nation reusable stainless steel and BPA free bottles. The proceeds go back to the foundation who then sends filtration units in return. The units provide healthy water for students and encourages the continued use of reusable bottles thus reducing plastic waste.
